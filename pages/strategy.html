Marketing Strategy

Proven marketing techniques for food businesses

Social Media Strategy

Leverage social media platforms to build brand awareness and engage customers.

Action Plan:

  1. Post high-quality food photography daily
  2. Run weekly promotions and contests
  3. Engage with customer comments
  4. Use relevant food hashtags
Recommended Posts: 7-10/week
Best Time to Post: 5-7 PM

Loyalty Program

Build customer retention through effective loyalty programs and repeat business incentives.

Program Options:

  • Points System: Earn points per dollar spent
  • Punch Card: 10th order free
  • Tiered Rewards: Bronze, Silver, Gold levels
  • Referral Program: Reward for bringing new customers

Email Marketing

Build an email list and create effective campaigns to drive repeat orders.

Campaign Ideas:

  • Welcome series for new customers
  • Weekly specials newsletter
  • Birthday discounts
  • Re-engagement campaigns

Supplier Management Tips

Negotiation Strategy

Always negotiate prices based on volume. Consider long-term contracts for stable pricing.

Quality Control

Implement a quality checklist for incoming ingredients. Reject substandard deliveries.

Backup Suppliers

Always have at least two suppliers for critical ingredients to avoid disruptions.

Growth Strategy

Scale your business and explore new opportunities

Month 1-3

Foundation Phase

Focus on perfecting operations, building customer base, and establishing brand presence.

  • Reach 50 regular customers
  • Achieve 4.0+ star rating
  • Break-even financially
Month 4-6

Growth Phase

Expand marketing efforts, introduce new menu items, and optimize operations.

  • Double customer base
  • Introduce 3-5 new menu items
  • Implement loyalty program
Month 7-12

Expansion Phase

Consider physical location, catering services, or delivery area expansion.

  • Explore second location
  • Launch catering services
  • Expand delivery radius
